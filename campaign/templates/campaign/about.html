About Valid

A platform designed to help founders validate B2B SaaS ideas with real SMEs — before building too much, too early.

Why Valid exists

Too many SaaS products are built without real validation. Founders spend months building features before speaking to actual users, leading to wasted time, resources, and failed products.

Valid exists to bridge that gap — connecting founders with real SME operators early, so validation happens before heavy development.

How it works

1. Founders create campaigns

Founders describe a problem, a proposed solution, and a validation goal.

2. SMEs discover campaigns

SME operators browse relevant campaigns based on their industry and role.

3. Real validation happens

SMEs request intros, and founders track conversations, pilots, and outcomes.

Who is Valid for?

Founders

Early-stage SaaS builders who want to validate ideas with real users before scaling development.

  • Create validation campaigns
  • Define target SME profiles
  • Track real-world interest and feedback
SMEs (Small and Medium-sized Enterprises)

Operators, managers, and decision-makers looking for tools that solve real business problems.

  • Discover early-stage solutions
  • Request introductions to founders
  • Influence product direction early

Key terms

Campaign

A structured validation page created by a founder describing a problem, solution, and target users.

Founder

A user who creates campaigns to validate SaaS ideas and connect with SMEs.

SME

A Small or Medium-sized Enterprise — typically the target customer for B2B SaaS solutions.

Intro Request

A signal from an SME expressing interest in a campaign and requesting contact with the founder.

Validation Goal

The measurable objective of a campaign (e.g., number of discovery calls, pilots, or signups).

What makes Valid different?

  • Focused on validation, not just exposure
  • Structured campaigns instead of generic listings
  • Direct signals of interest (intro requests)
  • Built for early-stage B2B SaaS, not mature products
